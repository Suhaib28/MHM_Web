About the Mesa Baseball Museum

Welcome to the Mesa Baseball Museum, a treasure trove of history celebrating Mesaâ€™s deep-rooted legacy in the sport of baseball. Founded in 1955, our museum provides visitors a closer look at the heroes of the game, the iconic moments that shaped the sport, and how Mesa became a central hub in baseball history.

The museum offers an immersive experience, with physical exhibits and an interactive mobile app that allows you to explore the stories of legendary baseball players, pivotal games, and unforgettable events that took place right here in Mesa.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve and share the incredible history of baseball in Mesa, Arizona, while promoting a greater appreciation for the game. Whether you're an avid baseball fan, a historian, or someone looking to learn more about this great sport, the Mesa Baseball Museum is a place to experience it all.
