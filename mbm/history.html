History of Mesa Baseball

Baseball has been an integral part of Mesaâ€™s culture for over a century. The first professional baseball game was played in Mesa in 1915, and since then, the city has played a pivotal role in shaping the sport. Our museum chronicles this rich history through extensive exhibits and artifacts.

Key Milestones

  • 1915: The founding of the Mesa Baseball League and the first professional game.
  • 1940s: The rise of the local players who went on to make it to the Major Leagues.
  • 1970s: Establishment of the Mesa Spring Training complex, attracting major league teams.

Notable Events

The museum also highlights the major games and events that took place in Mesa, including spring training games that saw teams like the Chicago Cubs and Chicago White Sox prepare for their season right here in our city.
